How It Works

  1. Invite Veil to Your Server
    Veil provides the /vote command needed for top.gg votes.
  2. Invite NitroVote
    NitroVote tracks votes and shows the monthly leaderboard.
  3. Use /vote on Veil
    Run the command and click the top.gg button.
  4. Vote on top.gg
    You can vote once every 12 hours.
  5. Track Your Rank
    Use /leaderboard to see who’s leading.

Eligibility & Prizes

  • Top 3 voters each month win a Discord Nitro subscription.
  • You must cast at least 30 votes during the month to qualify.
  • Ties are broken by the earliest to reach the tied total.
  • Votes are verified using top.gg webhooks—fraudulent votes are removed.
  • Winners are announced with the /winners command at the start of each month.
